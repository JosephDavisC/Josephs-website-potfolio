Cinema XXI Design

This project was created during my UI/UX bootcamp at StarCamp Academy, I learned about UX Research and UX Design, both of which are necessary for creating the UI of the project. UX Research involves studying user behavior, needs, and motivations in order to make products, services, and websites more user-friendly. UX Design focuses on creating positive experiences for the user through ease of use, efficiency, relevance, and overall enjoyment. With these concepts in mind, I began designing a cinema app based on XXI (Cinema 21) as a reference.
