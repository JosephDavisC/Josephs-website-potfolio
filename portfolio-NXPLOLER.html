Shell NXploler

For this activity, I was selected to be the leader and tasked with choosing three team members to attend a workshop. I chose Raja, Bennet, and Kiki to be on my team. We were also asked to come up with ideas related to Energy, Water, or Food , and my team chose water as our main topic. During the Shell NXplorer workshop, we learned how to develop plans and present our ideas using various tactics, as well as how to adapt to different situations. This was just a workshop and the main competition has not yet begun.
