Dino Marine

As an intern game developer at Starcamp / VR-Park, I had the chance to work on a new project called Dino Marine. My role involved writing the foundational scripts for the game, as well as adding subtitles and multiplayer capabilities. I was able to secure this internship position through the Unity Bootcamp I completed at the Starcamp Academy. The experience of working on Dino Marine was incredibly valuable and I'm grateful for the opportunity to contribute to such a unique project. For those interested in learning more about the project, there is a YouTube video available for desktop users on the left side of the page, and for mobile users at the top of the page.
